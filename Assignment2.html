Reflection

During the virtual talk held on December 5th, six speakers from different divisions at UTM Digital shared insights into strengthening academia-centric service delivery, data-driven digital ecosystems, data center construction, network management, security measures, and information system development. Subsequently, our visit to UTM Digital's main building, central for internet and electricity operations, provided a firsthand look at their well-equipped workspace, conference rooms, and secure data center under constant monitoring. Conversations with Dr. Aruslin Abdullah during the tour enriched our understanding of UTM's smart services and portal operations. The virtual discussion, coupled with the on-site experience, deepened our comprehension of UTM Digital's infrastructure, network, security, and system development. As computing students, this exposure bridged the gap between theory and real-world applications, motivating us to excel and make a positive impression on potential employers, including UTM Digital.
